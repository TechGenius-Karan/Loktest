Vision

At Lokgreen Energy, our vision is to lead the way in renewable energy and create a more sustainable future for all!

Lokgreen Energy is dedicated to leading the way in renewable energy to create a sustainable future for all. The company believes that renewable energy is vital for the planet's health and the well-being of people worldwide.
To achieve this vision - We build or buy renewable assets, solar and storage, that can generate or store clean electricity at a price that saves our customers money and produces a strong return for our investors. Our partner, Ztric Private Limited India, has been doing this for 7 years and Lok Green Energy is taking that expertise to a new level to address many more commercial and industrial customers. Our aim is to have 1 GW of solar and storage in operation in 15 years, helping to address India’s need for electricity and to make a difference in its environment.
Lokgreen Energy is committed to continuous improvement and innovation, aiming to become a leading renewable energy company that has a positive and lasting impact on people and communities, driving the transition to a cleaner, brighter future for all.
